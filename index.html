Disaster Risk Reduction and Management in the Philippines

Disaster Risk Reduction and Management (DRRM) in the Philippines refers to the systematic approach and strategies implemented to minimize the risks and vulnerabilities associated with natural and human-induced hazards. ICT plays a crucial role in DRRM in the Philippines. It enables enhanced communication, data collection and analysis, early warning systems,information sharing and coordination, as well as disaster preparedness and training.

My Role and Contribution as a Computer Programmer I

As a Computer Programmer I in the DRRM Division, my contribution will involve developing and maintaining software applications and tools that support DRRM activities. I will collaborate with stakeholders, ensure system security, provide technical support and stay updated with emerging technologies in DRRM and ICT.
